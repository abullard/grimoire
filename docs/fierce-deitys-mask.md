# Fierce Deity’s Mask — Brief Technical Reference (Majora’s Mask)

## Overview

The **Fierce Deity’s Mask** is a powerful transformation mask in _The Legend of Zelda: Majora’s Mask_, granting Link enhanced strength, ranged energy attacks, and an adult-like form. It is primarily intended for the game’s final boss encounter and is not usable in most standard overworld or dungeon scenarios.

---

## Core Characteristics

- **Transformation Type:** Deity-like adult Link.
- **Primary Ability:** Charged sword beams from each swing.
- **Combat Profile:** High damage, extended reach, trivializes most boss encounters.
- **Usage Restrictions:** Only usable in boss rooms.

---

## High-Level Quest Context (Non-Detailed)

- Obtained as a **reward for completing the masked child trials on the Moon**.
- Requires possession of **all major transformation masks** and **a full set of other masks**, which are traded during the trials.
- The quest is tied to the narrative theme of sacrifice, identity, and mask symbolism but is mechanically straightforward.

---

## Mechanics & Boss Interaction

- Fierce Deity Link can fire **energy projectiles** that lock on to enemies.
- Dramatically reduces difficulty of the final battle.
- High magic consumption encourages players to manage magic resources.
